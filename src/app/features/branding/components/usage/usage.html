05 — Usage Guidelines

Logo Usage Standards

✓

Minimum Size

Maintain a minimum logo height of 24px for digital applications and 8mm for print.

✓

Clear Space

Keep clear space equal to the central triangle around all sides of the logo.

✓

Dark Backgrounds

Preferred application. Use the standard logo version on dark surfaces.

✓

Light Backgrounds

Use the light-adapted logo variant to maintain visibility and contrast.

✕

Do Not Distort

Never stretch, skew or alter the proportions of the logo.

✕

Do Not Recolor

Do not replace the approved brand colors with unapproved alternatives.
