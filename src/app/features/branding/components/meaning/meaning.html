01 — Symbol

The Meaning Behind the Mark

△

Upward Triangle — Shiva

The outer gold triangle points upward — aspiration, growth and forward momentum. It represents consciousness, precision and structure.

▽

Downward Triangle — Shakti

The descending triangle represents foundation, execution and grounded delivery. Engineering discipline that transforms ideas into reality.

△

Inner Triangle — Product Layer

A smaller focused triangle representing clarity, refinement and product excellence.

●

Bindu — Origin Point

The central bindu represents the source, the founding idea and clarity before creation.
